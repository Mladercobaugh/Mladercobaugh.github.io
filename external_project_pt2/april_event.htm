National Pet Day
Thursday April 11th, 2024

Come and Join us for National Pet Day. Bring your pets, family friends or just yourself to celebrate man's best friend. We will have little pet packages at registration sign up. There will also be water check points for you and your pets as well throughout your chosen race distance.

Registration Packet Bib pick-up: 7:30am

Race Begins: 10:00am

Race options: 3k, 5k and 10k

Cost at Check-in: $10 registration fee Individuals.
$15 for team of two.
